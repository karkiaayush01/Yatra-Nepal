Yatra@Mustang

10-17 April

Details
Pricing
FAQ

A fascinating travel awaits you! This April, we have decided to travel to Mustang, the heart of tour spots in Nepal. Join us to become a member in this journey and enjoy the chilly atmosphere.

Registration open till 5th April (Slots Available: 12)

Details

This 8-day-long trip commences from Kathmandu. We will be riding to the following routes in order:

  • Day 1: Kathmandu To Pokhara
  • Day 2: Pokhara To Kalopani
  • Day 3: Kalopani To Shamar
  • Day 4: Shamar To Lo-Mangthang Village
  • Day 5: Lo-Mangthang Village To Kagbeni
  • Day 6: Kagbeni To Tatopani
  • Day 7: Tatopani To Pokhara
  • Day 8: Pokhara To Kathmandu

Lodging and food between the days are all managed by the team

Pricing

The pricing for the trip with your own bike is Rs. 12000. This includes all the fooding, lodging and safety benefits.

You can check and rent bikes from our rentals section as per your requirement as well.

FAQ

Q.

How many people are generally in a trip?

A:

Our trip generally consists of 15-20 members. The maximum number of members we accept for a trip is 25 excluding the team members.

Q.

How safe are the trips?

A:

The trips are very safe. We have many professionals in out team that make sure that all the trip conditions are optimal and that includes both natural conditions and the trip’s. If the trip conditions are not safe, we delay the trip as well.

Q.

The slots are full, can I get an extra slot?

A:

While our trip usually has extra slots, we cannot say for sure. If you see that there are no slots available, you can email us at info@yatranepal.com and we will inform you if we can offer you a slot.
